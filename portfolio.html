Building Your Portfolio

Making a Portfolio That Shows Off Your Best

Making a portfolio may seem daunting at first, but the UMSI CDO team is here to help take this important task and break it down into simple steps.

Need feedback? You can always meet with a CDO Career Coach to review your portfolio.

How to Build a Successful Portfolio: A Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Make a Copy of UMSI's Very Own Portfolio Checklist: Use these guidelines to make starting your portfolio easiser.
  2. View Sample Portfolios Online: Using websites such as Cofolios.com to view sample portfolios gives you inspiration on how to construct your portfolio.
  3. Select a Portfolio-Building Website to Begin: Many people choose to use a website-builder to make their portolio. Choose a builder that you like and then select a plan that works for your budget. Some common website builders include Squarespace, Webflow, Wordpress, and Adobe Portfolio (Free with Adobe account). You can also learn to code your own website!
  4. Visit the UMSI CDO Course on Canvas: All UMSI Undergraduate and Graduate students have access to a vital resource from the CDO on Canvas that includes links and resources for building the best portfolio.
